# EU Depositary Industry — Reference Documentation

Companion reading list for `EU_Depositary_Industry_Report.docx`. Mix of locally
mirrored documents (where the source allows scraping) and authoritative URLs
(where it does not).

## Mirrored locally in this folder

| File | What it is | Why it matters |
|---|---|---|
| `ESMA_Opinion_Asset_Segregation_2017.pdf` | ESMA Opinion 34-45-277 (Jul 2017) | Defines the minimum-3-segregated-account expectation at the level of the depositary's delegate (sub-custodian) and addresses the treatment of CSDs as delegates vs. infrastructure. |
| `ESMA_Letter_AIFMD_Review_2020.pdf` | ESMA letter to the European Commission on the AIFMD review (Aug 2020) | The blueprint that fed the AIFMD II proposal: tightens delegation supervision, harmonises liquidity tools, addresses loan origination. |
| `Linklaters_AIFMD_Depositaries.html` | Linklaters legal briefing | Practitioner-grade summary of AIFMD depositary scope, delegation, liability and discharge. |
| `Oxford_LawBlog_Madoff_Depositary_Liability.html` | Oxford Business Law Blog | Academic post-mortem of the Madoff feeder fund litigation and what it implied for UCITS investor rights against the depositary. |
| `AIMA_ESMA_Asset_Segregation_Opinion.html` | AIMA briefing | Industry interpretation of the ESMA segregation opinion — useful because written for fund managers (the depositary's clients). |

## Reading order suggestion

1. Start with the **EUR-Lex summary** + **ESMA Opinion 34-45-277** to get the regulatory frame.
2. Read **Linklaters AIFMD Depositaries** for a practitioner translation.
3. Read **Oxford Madoff post-mortem** for the foundational liability case.
4. Read **AMF H2O sanction** + **Delano H2O CACEIS** for the most recent and most relevant European depositary-litigation event.
5. Skim **KPMG Depositary Survey 2025** + **EY Costs and charges** for market structure.
6. Read **AIFMD II** to anchor the regulatory direction of travel.
